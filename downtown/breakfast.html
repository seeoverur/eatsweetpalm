Downtown Charleston To Go Breakfast

Fuel your morning on the Street without the wait. Whether you need a downtown quick breakfast before work or a hearty start to a day of exploring, Sweet Palm serves up chef-inspired sandwiches, wraps, and bowls made fresh daily.

Why We Are the Best Downtown Charleston Breakfast Spot

In a city known for its culinary scene, finding a high-quality downtown Charleston breakfast that doesn't require a reservation can be tough. At Sweet Palm, we bridge the gap between a sit-down brunch and a fast-food stop. We use real ingredients—like local dairy and artisan breads—to create breakfast sandwiches and bowls that are ready when you are.
