The Chef Behind the Wrap
Jonathan Roth
Owner & Chef, Sweet Palm Coffee & Food

Born in Seattle, raised in New York, and trained at the Culinary Institute of America — Jonathan Roth spent years living and working in Switzerland, where he honed his craft in professional kitchens and opened multiple hospitality businesses before selling them and relocating to the U.S.

After a brief stint in Miami, Roth and his wife Aylin chose Charleston. In October 2025, they opened Sweet Palm at 471 King Street with a singular focus: a café where both the food and drinks are made from scratch, with the same care and precision a trained chef brings to every plate.

The Chicken Caesar Wrap — built on house-made dressing, fresh-grilled chicken, and real parmesan — is the dish that best shows what that philosophy tastes like.
