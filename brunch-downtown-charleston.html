Every brunch plate at Sweet Palm is made fresh when you order it. No heat lamps, no pre-packaged shortcuts — just real food that tastes like someone actually cooked it for you. Because they did.

Chicken Caesar Wrap

Breakfast Burrito

Scrambled eggs, melted cheddar, seasoned potatoes, and your choice of bacon or sausage wrapped and ready. The go-to King Street brunch plate when you want something serious.

Avocado Toast

Brunch Salads
