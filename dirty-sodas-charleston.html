The Trend Explained

What Is a Dirty Soda?

A dirty soda is a customizable fizzy drink built on your choice of soda, flavored syrup, a splash of cream or coconut milk, and fresh mix-ins. Originally a Utah phenomenon, dirty sodas have taken Charleston by storm — and we're bringing them to King Street at Sweet Palm.

Every drink is built on four layers:

Sparkling Soda Base House Flavored Syrup Cream or Coconut Milk Fresh Mix-Ins & Garnish

The result? A drink that's sweet, fizzy, creamy, and totally yours.

How It Works

Your Drink, Your Way

01
Pick Your Base

Sprite, Topo Chico, Cherry Cola, Dr Pepper, cream soda — or ask us what's in the fridge today.

02
Choose a Syrup

Pick from our rotating house-made syrups: peach, hibiscus, blueberry, vanilla, coconut, and more.

03
Add the Cream

Heavy cream, half-and-half, or coconut milk — the "dirty" layer that makes it magic.

Fresh fruit, espresso shots, garnishes, extra syrup — make it yours. We love a special request.
