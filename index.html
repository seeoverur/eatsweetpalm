A Downtown Charleston Food Destination

Your King Street Café

Sweet Palm Coffee & Café is Downtown Charleston's favorite spot for fresh café food and handcrafted coffee on King Street. Whether you're a College of Charleston student powering through a study session, a professional hunting for the perfect working lunch, or a visitor exploring the French Quarter and Historic District, Sweet Palm is your local food and coffee anchor.

We believe a great café meal starts with real ingredients. Our chicken caesar wraps, breakfast burritos, and BLT wraps are made fresh to order — no shortcuts, no shortcuts, no heat lamps. Just good food that tastes like it was made with care, because it was. Every dish is designed to be satisfying whether you're eating in or grabbing it on the go up King Street.

"We're not just a coffee shop — we're the café where Downtown Charleston comes to eat, work, and connect. Every wrap, burrito, and latte is made with Lowcountry pride."

Located at 471 King Street, we serve the heart of the Charleston peninsula — from Cannonborough and Radcliffeborough to the French Quarter and Historic District. Walk in from Upper King or Church Street and you'll feel it immediately: a warm, coastal-inspired space that's equal parts productive and relaxing.
